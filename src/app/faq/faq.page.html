FAQ FAQ What is this?

DungeonBuddy is a school project. I was tasked with building an app from scratch in Angular using Ionic and other tools. What you see here is what the result of this project.

Firebase was used for hosting and database services.

DND5api was used for getting data relevant to character race and class.
